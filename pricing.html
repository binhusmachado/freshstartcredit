Pricing Questions

When do I pay?

For per-deletion pricing, you pay after we confirm an item has been removed from your credit report. You'll receive an invoice with proof of deletion. Package deals are paid upfront but include our money-back guarantee.

What if nothing gets deleted?

If no items are removed within 90 days, you owe nothing for per-deletion pricing. For package deals, we offer a partial refund based on work completed.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The price you see is the price you pay. We cover all postage and processing costs. Credit reports from the bureaus are free if you haven't received them in the last 12 months.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. If you start with per-deletion and want to switch to a package deal (or vice versa), just let us know. We'll apply any payments already made toward your new plan.
